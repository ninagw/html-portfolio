The best books I read in 2023

My top 3 picks.

Butter

Placed in Japan. Really enjoyed diving in the culture and the writing of the author.

Der Papierpalast

Perfect summer novel! Dreamed away by the scenery at the sea. Started me thinking about my future goals and expanded my vision board.

Circe

Different read for me because it's placed in the world of gods which was new to me. But thanks to the writing skills of the author I could really live in this world and feel the struggle of Circe.
